Innovation

Sustainability

Sustainability is the idea of using resources in a way that ensures they can be maintained over time, rather than depleted.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is composed of public and private physical structures such as roads, railways, bridges, airports, public transit systems, tunnels, water supply, sewers, electrical grids, and telecommunications.

Empowerment

To answer the question, 'What is empowerment?' means giving more rights or freedom to a group of people.

With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.
